Joining, Setting Up & Taking Part

How do I create a BuzzChat account?

  1. Download the BuzzChat app from your app store.
  2. Open the app and tap on "Sign Up."
  3. Enter your first name, last name, email address, desired username, country code, and mobile number.
  4. Create a strong password.
  5. Verify your phone number by entering the code sent to you.
  6. Tap "Sign Up" to finalize the process.

What does my BuzzChat profile show others?

Your profile displays your name, username, profile picture (optional), number of posts ("Buzzes"), number of people you follow ("Connections"), and number of followers ("Connected"). It's your digital identity on BuzzChat!

How do I connect with friends and others on BuzzChat?

There are three main ways to connect:

  • Invite your friends: Share your username or a link to your profile. They can then search for you and initiate a connection.
  • Through search: Use the search bar to find people by their name, username, email address, or phone number (if you have it).
  • Through likes and shared interests: Connect with people who like your posts and engage with content that resonates with you.

What's the timeline all about?

The timeline is your personalized feed showing posts from people you connect with. It's where you stay updated on their activities, share your thoughts, engage in conversations, and build relationships within the BuzzChat community. Think of it as your central hub for connection and interaction.

How do I create a post on BuzzChat?

  1. Go to your timeline and tap the round blue button in the lower right corner.
  2. Type your message in the text box. You have a character limit that can be increased by earning Buzz Bucks (more on that later!).
  3. Express yourself freely but remember to be respectful and avoid offensive language. Our AI Moderation system ensures a safe and positive environment for everyone.
  4. Want to add more than just text? Use the three interactive buttons to upload photos, videos, or GIFs to make your posts even more engaging.

What if I need inspiration for a post?

BuzzChat has you covered! Our AI-powered content generator can help you craft the perfect post. Just provide a brief description of what you want to say or the kind of image you have in mind, and our AI will work its magic. You can even select the tone of your message (Formal, Casual, Confident, Humorous) to match your style.

What are the different ways I can interact with a post?

Here are five ways to engage with posts you find interesting:

  1. Like: Tap the heart icon to show your appreciation for a post. Tap again to unlike.
  2. Comment: Share your thoughts, opinions, or questions in the comments section. You can also use this to report any concerns to our moderators.
  3. Rebuzz: Like something you see? Use the arrows button to instantly share the post on your timeline and with your connections.
  4. Fact Check: Not sure about the accuracy of a post? Tap the bulb icon to initiate a fact-check.
  5. More Menu: The three horizontal dots in the upper right corner of a post give you more options like copying the post, reporting it, sharing it outside BuzzChat, saving it to your bookmarks, and viewing the number of likes it has received.

Can I see who viewed my post?

The eye logo button on your post shows the total number of views, but it doesn't reveal the identity of those viewers. Your privacy matters to us!

Buzz Bucks and Commerce

What are Buzz Bucks, and what can I do with them?

Buzz Bucks are the virtual currency of BuzzChat, and they unlock a world of possibilities within the app. You need them to access advanced features like AI content generators, engaging chatbots, and the creative Artists Corner. Think of them as your gateway to the most exciting aspects of the BuzzChat experience.

How do I earn Buzz Bucks?

Earning Buzz Bucks is as easy as being an active member of the community! Here's how:

  • Engage, engage, engage! The more you post, comment, like, and interact with others, the more Buzz Bucks you earn. It's all about contributing to the vibrant BuzzChat ecosystem.
  • Spread the love (and the Bucks!). Received some Buzz Bucks from a fellow user? That's the spirit of community! You can do the same by transferring your Buzz Bucks to friends and connections.

Why are Buzz Bucks so important on BuzzChat?

Think of Buzz Bucks as a symbol of trust and quality within our community. They help us ensure that the services offered are genuine and reliable. They also help prevent misuse of the amazing tools and resources we offer. By using Buzz Bucks, you're contributing to a safer and more valuable experience for everyone.

How do I check my Buzz Bucks balance and manage them?

Head over to your "Coins" page, your personal dashboard for all things Buzz Bucks. Here's what you can do:

  • Check your balance: See how many Buzz Bucks you have and their equivalent value in your local currency.
  • Redeem codes: We often send out redemption codes for free Buzz Bucks through email. Don't miss out on these bonuses!
  • Find your Buzz ID: This unique ID is how other users can send you Buzz Bucks directly.
  • Transfer Buzz Bucks: Share the wealth! Enter the Buzz ID of the person you want to send Buzz Bucks to and the amount you'd like to transfer.

What is Buzz Commerce, and how does it work?

Buzz Commerce is your platform to showcase your business and connect with potential customers within the BuzzChat community. It's like having your own digital shop window!

Here's the catch—we don't handle the actual transactions. Think of us as the bridge between you and your customers. When someone is ready to buy, we direct them to your external e-commerce platform, whether it's Jumia, eBay, Amazon, or your own website.

How do I set up my Buzz Commerce shop?

Ready to start selling? It's simple! Just follow these steps:

  1. Go to your "Settings" and select "Buzz Commerce."
  2. Fill out the registration form with your business information, website, and links to your sales channels.
  3. Our team will review your application to ensure authenticity and prevent scams. You'll receive an email within a week regarding approval.
  4. Once approved, you can start creating your shop!

Need to update your information or make changes to your listings? No problem! Just contact BuzzChat Support, and we'll gladly assist you.

What if I want to expand my e-commerce reach even further?

We've got you covered! Select "Request Commerce Distribution" within Buzz Commerce to start a conversation with our e-commerce team. We'll explore tailored solutions to maximize your business's visibility and reach a wider audience within the BuzzChat ecosystem.

  • Business Tools: Access the Business Idea Generator, Name Picker, Business Plan Analysis, and other valuable tools to help you launch or grow your business.
  • Artist's Corner: This is where you'll find the tools to create digital art using AI. Turn your words into visual masterpieces and share your creations with the world!
  • Account Review Generator: Get personalized suggestions on how to maximize your BuzzChat experience. Our AI analyzes your activity and provides insights to help you connect with more people, increase your visibility, and get the most out of the platform.
  • Support: Need help with something? Our friendly support team is here to assist you via live chat. Get answers to your questions and troubleshoot any issues you might encounter.
  • Advertise: Reach a wider audience by creating targeted ads on BuzzChat. Choose your target demographic, set your budget, and track your ad performance all within the app.

Your settings are your key to a personalized and secure BuzzChat experience. Explore the options and make BuzzChat your own!
